About Our School

Our History

Established in 19 June 1984, St. Thomas HSS has been a beacon of knowledge in Idukki. We are committed to fostering academic excellence.

Our Mission

To provide quality education that nurtures the intellectual, social, and moral growth of every student.

Location

Located in the heart of Erattayar, surrounded by nature's beauty, providing a serene environment for learning.
